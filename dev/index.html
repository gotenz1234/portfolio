Artifical Intelligence Prompt Engineering

October 2024 IT, AI, Creative Client: Personal Project

About the Project

This is a personal project in exploring and pushing the boundaries of AI. The term AI has been mainstream but it is defilied by the connotation that AI can only produces uncanny valley-esque imagery.
This project aims to blur the lines between reality and AI as we continues to explore the endless posibilities.

Results Achieved

The tools employed in this project, while not the most recent, still yield highly impressive results. The primary limitation of the current system lies in the AI's inability to generate legible text.
However, this issue can be easily addressed with human intervention, as the AI-generated art is intended to accelerate the creative process rather than replace it entirely.

Project Details

  • Client: Personal Project
  • Duration: Ongoing
  • Tools Used: Deepseek, CivitAI, Stable Diffusion, ChatGPT, Qwen, LLama

The modern sorcery

Originally captured at Sensoji Temple, the photograph has been reimagined through the application of img2img technology, resulting in a strikingly eerie composition.
The characters within the scene now appear to gaze directly at the viewer, intensifying the sense of immersion and intrigue. Example of applying it in a real-life situation: Rapid prototyping of a certain product.
In the below example, I have generated an image of a Coke can in a photo studio setting. We can then remove the details from the Can and add in our own details to speed up our workflow.
