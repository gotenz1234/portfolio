Online Website Rebranding Strategy

March 2024 Marketing, Social Media Client: Local Beauty Clinic

About the Project

The client sought a comprehensive brand overhaul to better align with a younger demographic. This included a complete redesign of their website, transitioning from a traditional "Balinese" theme to a modern, minimalist aesthetic.
The previous design was perceived as dark and uninviting, and the goal was to create an engaging, user-friendly experience that would attract a younger, more vibrant audience.
Through strategic design choices, the new look was crafted to be both visually appealing and welcoming, enhancing the overall customer experience and supporting the clientâ€™s evolving business goals.

Key Features

  • Conducted thorough market research to better understand the desires of the new target audience.
  • Adopted a user-centric design tailored to engage the younger generation, focusing on a clean, modern aesthetic to resonate with their preferences.
  • Revitalized the website by shifting the target market from an older demographic to a younger, more dynamic audience.
  • Based on findings, I transitioned the website from a traditional Balinese theme to a minimalist design that speaks to the tastes of younger users.

Results Achieved

The campaign successfully increased brand awareness and customer engagement, leading to a significant boost in foot traffic and online booking.

Project Details

  • Client: Local Beauty Clinic
  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Tools Used: Canva, Wordpress, Stable Diffusion
  • Platforms: Instagram, Facebook
